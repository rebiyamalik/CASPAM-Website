Introduction

Our study programs focus on critical thinking, applying fundamental concepts, analyzing real-life problems, idea generation, model development, algorithm writing, and solution understanding. We offer BS, MS/MPhil and PhD in Mathematics together with modern areas like AI, data science and computational mathematics so our graduates can pursue diverse careers such as data scientists, actuaries and analysts.

Vision & Mission

Vision: To attain heights of excellence through quality academic teaching, learning and research that develop individuals, industry and society intellectually, professionally and ethically.

Mission: Provide outcome-based education, promote interdisciplinary research, employ innovative teaching methods, and build industry linkages to prepare graduates for real-world challenges.
