Programs Offered

BS Program in Mathematics (Morning & Evening)

Eligibility: Intermediate Examination (Pre-Engineering) or an equivalent examination recognized by the University with Mathematics as an elective subject.

BS (5th Semester) Program

Eligibility: Applicants must meet at least one of the criteria: B.A/B.Sc with Mathematics A & B with 45% marks, B.A/B.Sc with general Mathematics (45% aggregate), ADS with Mathematics major, or ADS with at least four Mathematics courses including calculus. Applicants under some criteria may be required to pass deficiency courses.

MS/M.Phil. Program in Mathematics

Eligibility: M.Sc./BS (4-Year) Mathematics with at least 50% marks under annual system or CGPA 2.5/4.0 or 2.8/5.0 under semester system and qualifying the admission test as per university policy.

Ph.D. Program in Mathematics

Eligibility: MS/M.Phil. in Mathematics with a minimum CGPA of 3.0/4.0 or First Division under annual system, or equivalent degree in related fields with required CGPA, and qualifying the admission test.
