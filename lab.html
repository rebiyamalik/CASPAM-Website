Computer Centre

The Computer Centre at CASPAM serves as a hub for computational research, data analysis, and scientific computing. It is equipped with advanced hardware, software and networking infrastructure to support advanced mathematical modeling, simulation, and analysis. The centre supports student projects, research activities and provides access to specialized scientific software and high-performance workstations.
